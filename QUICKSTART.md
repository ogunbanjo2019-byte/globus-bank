# Quick Start Guide - Global Bank v2.0

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Open the Application
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. No installation needed!

### Step 2: Create an Account
1. Click the **"Sign Up"** button
2. Enter your details:
   - Full Name: Your name
   - Email: Your email address
   - Password: Create a strong password
   - Security PIN: 4-digit number (e.g., 1234)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be redirected to login

### Step 3: Login
1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Login"**
3. Welcome to your dashboard!

## 💰 Making a Transfer

### Local Bank Transfer
1. Click **"Transfer Funds"** or **"Local Bank Transfer"**
2. Fill in bank details:
   - Bank Name
   - Account Number
   - Routing Number
   - Account Type
3. Enter transfer amount
4. Enter your 4-digit PIN
5. Review the fee breakdown
6. Click **"Submit Transfer Request"**
7. **Important**: You'll receive payment instructions in chat
8. Your balance is NOT deducted yet
9. Pay the amount shown in instructions
10. Transfer processes after payment

### International Transfer
1. Click **"International Transfer"**
2. Enter recipient details:
   - Recipient Name
   - Bank Name
   - Country
   - Recipient Currency
3. Enter amount in USD
4. Enter your 4-digit PIN
5. Review conversion and fees
6. Click **"Submit International Transfer"**
7. Receive payment instructions in chat
8. Pay the total amount
9. Transfer processes after payment

## 🔑 Important Notes

### Manual Payment System
- Your account balance is **NOT** automatically deducted
- You receive payment instructions via chat
- You pay manually from your hand/wallet
- Transfer processes after payment is received

### Security PIN
- Required for all transactions
- 4 digits only
- Keep it secret
- You set it during signup

### Fees
- **Local Transfers**: 2% of amount
- **International Transfers**: 3% of amount
- **Deposits**: No fees
- **Currency Conversion**: No fees

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Why isn't my balance deducted?**
A: This is the manual payment system. You pay manually, then the transfer processes.

**Q: Where are my payment instructions?**
A: Check the chat widget (💬) at the bottom right. Instructions are sent there automatically.

**Q: How long do transfers take?**
A: Local transfers are instant after payment. International transfers take 1-2 business days.

**Q: Can I convert currencies?**
A: Yes! Use the Currency Converter section in your dashboard.

**Q: What if I forget my PIN?**
A: You'll need to create a new account. PINs cannot be reset in this demo.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: This is a demo app using browser storage. In production, use proper security.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Can't login
**Solution**: Check email and password. Make sure you created an account first.

**Problem**: PIN not working
**Solution**: PIN must be exactly 4 digits. No letters or special characters.

**Problem**: Transfer not submitted
**Solution**: Fill all required fields and enter correct PIN.

**Problem**: Chat not showing
**Solution**: Click the chat bubble (💬) at bottom right to open.

**Problem**: Currency conversion not working
**Solution**: Make sure you entered an amount and selected currencies.
