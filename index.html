Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum account balance?

You can open an account with a minimum balance of $10,000. This ensures we can provide premium services and support.

How do I link my bank account?

Navigate to the "Link Bank Account" section in your dashboard. Select your bank from our comprehensive list of USA and international banks, and follow the verification process.

How do I transfer funds?

Simply navigate to the Transfer section in your dashboard, select your destination, and follow the secure transfer process. You'll receive payment instructions to complete the transfer manually.

How does manual payment work?

With our manual payment system, your account balance is not automatically deducted. Instead, you'll receive detailed payment instructions via chat or email, and you can pay directly from your hand/wallet.

Can I convert currencies?

Yes! Our currency converter supports conversion between USD and multiple international currencies including EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, and AUD with real-time exchange rates.

Is my account secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption, multi-factor authentication, and advanced fraud detection to protect your account.

What are withdrawal fees?

Withdrawal fees help us maintain our secure infrastructure, fraud prevention systems, and 24/7 customer support. Fees vary based on withdrawal method and amount. Contact support for specific fee details.
