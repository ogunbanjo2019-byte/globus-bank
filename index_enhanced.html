About Global Bank

Founded in 2005, Global Bank has grown from a small local institution to a worldwide leader in financial services. Our mission is to provide accessible, secure, and innovative banking solutions to everyone, everywhere.

With over 50 million customers across 120 countries, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in digital finance, ensuring your money works as hard as you do.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

Simply click the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, and your account will be ready in minutes.

Are international transfers instant?

Most international transfers are processed within 24 hours, though some may take up to 3 business days depending on the destination country.

Is my money safe?

Yes, all deposits are insured up to $250,000 per account through our global banking partners.
